ABOUT US

The First Fuel Saver of India

Save Fuel, Save Nature, Save Money

Shreenivas Innovations Pvt. Ltd. was started by Mr. Deepak Rane in the year 2010. With lot of research and hard work starting from 2008, Shreenivas Innovations was born. Mr. Deepak Rane is a nature lover and was interested in doing something that would save the environment from pollution.

He took the help of Ayurveda and under his father's Mr. Prakash Rane's guidance started working on the development of Bio Additives and then Bio Fuel products. After the immense research of 5 years helped Mr. Rane to invent the best formulation of Petrol and Diesel additive that saved the fuel up to 40% and also increased the life of engines and other parts related to it. The perfect formula was invented after multiple experiments and it was ready to be launched in the market in the year 2020.

COMPANY DETAILS

Mr. Rane still continues doing research daily to manufacture more products for conserving our economical fuel for the country's growth. He wants to set a global trade mark in every country economical and natural with lesser the carbon footprint by boosting Green Fuel Efficiency.

The company now serves Auto and Industrial including oils and greases. Today, we have dealers and distributors networks throughout Maharashtra and Goa. Also, we have their distributors in many other states of India.

In 2013, Mr. Rane invented the remanufacturing process of Biofuel with the help of waste plastic. This process turns waste plastic into Biofuel as well as highly stored to be used by the consumers and large scale industries.
